A bit about me.

My name is Kerwin Lamberth. I moved to the United States in 2003 after living in Durban, South Africa for 8 years. I spent my youth in South Carolina attending school. While there, I spent my highschool years working at a movie theater building customer service experience. After highschool, I started working at Amazon Warehouses while I searched for direction in my life. In 2017, I entered the United States Marine Corps, which I would serve in for 5 years. In the military I lived in several different states, from North Carolina, all the way to California. The military gave me the experience and the discipline I needed to move forward in my life. With my newfound skills and hardened mind, I am now attending college at Western Washinginton University in Bellingham,WA; where I am aiming to major in computer science.
